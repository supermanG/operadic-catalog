\section{Lean 4 axiom footprint}\label{sec:axioms}

All theorems in \texttt{lean/OperadicCatalog.lean} depend only on the
standard Lean foundation axioms:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \texttt{propext} (propositional extensionality)
  \item \texttt{Classical.choice} (axiom of choice)
  \item \texttt{Quot.sound} (quotient soundness)
\end{itemize}
No project-specific axioms are used.
